Kharkiv

My favorite city that I have visited is Kharkiv. It is the second-largest city in Ukraine and a place full of history, culture, and life. I was impressed by its wide streets, beautiful squares, and modern architecture combined with historical buildings. One of the most remarkable spots is Freedom Square, which is one of the largest city squares in Europe. Kharkiv is also known for its many universities and young, dynamic atmosphere. But now during the war, things have changed. However, even now, I was impressed cleanliness of the city I enjoyed walking through its green parks, visiting cozy cafés, and feeling the welcoming spirit of the people. This city left me with many bright memories and a strong desire to come back again.

P.S. If I was asked where I'd live, I'd definitely say, that it's Kharkiv, because it's the best city not only in Ukraine, but in the whole world.
